# Q-RoPE Internal Positioning Memo v1

## Status
Q-RoPE should currently be positioned internally as:
- a legitimate research initiative
- methodologically rigorous
- technically inconclusive for practical NISQ advantage

## What has value
- coherent relative-phase method framing
- strong protocol and reproducibility discipline
- real remote execution feasibility on Quandela and IBM
- clear empirical boundary-setting

## What has not been shown
- stable performance advantage
- robust cross-provider agreement
- a successful next-generation local redesign path

## Internal recommendation
Treat the current repo as:
- retained internal research infrastructure
- a restart-capable asset
- not an active publication candidate under the current evidence

## Safe internal summary
“Q-RoPE remains conceptually interesting and operationally well-developed, but current evidence does not support a strong positive result. The project should be preserved internally and only reactivated technically if a materially different hypothesis is identified.”

## Unsafe internal shortcut
Do not summarize this as:
- “it failed completely”

That is too blunt and technically inaccurate.

Also do not summarize it as:
- “we are close to proving NISQ usefulness”

That is too optimistic relative to the evidence.

## Bottom line
The right internal framing is:
- credible research asset
- null-to-inconclusive current outcome
- preserve, do not hype
