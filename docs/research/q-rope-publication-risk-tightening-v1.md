# Q-RoPE Publication Risk Tightening v1

## Purpose
Translate the matched remote packet into explicit publication-safe claim boundaries.

## Remote-evidence-driven allowed claims
1. We demonstrate real remote execution of the Q-RoPE program on both a photonic provider path and an IBM Runtime comparator path.
2. We show that remote behavior on the current matched slice is seed-sensitive and provider-dependent.
3. We show that the current remote evidence is sufficient to bound claims, not to support a simple stable superiority narrative.
4. We show that direct photonic cloud execution is feasible under funded provider access.

## Remote-evidence-driven disallowed claims
1. "`V3` consistently outperforms baselines on remote backends."
2. "Q-RoPE is robust across providers."
3. "The remote packet validates hardware-style superiority."
4. "Provider differences are minor implementation noise."
5. "Current remote evidence generalizes beyond the present dataset and slice policy."

## Candidate overclaims to reject in manuscript or pitch form
1. "Q-RoPE wins on hardware."
2. "Q-RoPE is the best positional encoding for NISQ transformers."
3. "Photonic and superconducting backends agree on the ranking."
4. "Remote execution confirms the theoretical advantage."

## Recommended publication framing
- Primary framing:
  - Q-RoPE is a relative-phase positional encoding proposal with a coherent baseline taxonomy and a reproducible hybrid evaluation program.
- Empirical framing:
  - Current remote evidence demonstrates feasibility and instability, not stable advantage.
- Reviewer-safe phrasing:
  - "In our current matched remote packet, results are strongly seed- and provider-dependent."
  - "These data support feasibility and expose boundary conditions, rather than establishing universal remote gains."

## Recommended manuscript posture
- Keep the main manuscript centered on:
  - novelty framing,
  - method definition,
  - ablation design,
  - reproducibility,
  - bounded empirical observations
- Treat remote results as:
  - real and valuable,
  - but supplementary and cautionary rather than triumphal

## Bottom line
- The project remains publishable in a constrained form.
- The remote packet strengthens rigor by preventing overclaiming.
- The correct publication move is narrower, not louder.
