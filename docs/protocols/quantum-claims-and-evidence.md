# Quantum Claims and Evidence Protocol

## Purpose
Set a strict evidence discipline for QRoPE technical claims.

## Source hierarchy
1. Primary: peer-reviewed papers, arXiv preprints, official code/docs, reproducible datasets.
2. Secondary: high-quality surveys, benchmarks, or derivative explainers.
3. Tertiary: informal summaries (only for orientation, not final claims).

## Minimum evidence requirements
- High-impact claims require at least one primary source.
- Claims that influence architecture or research prioritization should have two independent sources when possible.
- If no source exists, claim must be marked `Inferred for repo` with uncertainty noted.

## Citation rules
- Cite exact artifact identifiers (DOI, arXiv ID, version tag, commit hash) where available.
- Include access date for web sources.
- Keep citations in the story `Evidence and references` section and in epic evidence logs.

## Contradiction handling
- Log contradictory findings explicitly.
- Route as `Revise` or `Escalate` in story notes when contradictions affect high-impact decisions.
